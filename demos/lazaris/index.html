Our Story

A bakery that
became a bar

Lazaris started as a one-man sourdough operation in 2018. The oven was too small. The space was too big. So we added coffee. Then wine. Then meze. Then more chairs.

Today we're a bakery until 11am, a café until 6pm, and a wine bar until late. The through-line: everything is made here, from the bread to the playlist.
