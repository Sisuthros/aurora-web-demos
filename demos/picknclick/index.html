About

Started with one buggy.
Now we're 745 reviews deep.

Pick n Click began in 2019 when two brothers bought a used buggy and posted it on Facebook Marketplace. The phone didn't stop ringing. Today we have 24 vehicles, three trail guides, and the highest-rated rental outfit in Larnaca.

Why? Because we don't just hand you keys. We give you a map, a helmet, a cooler of water, and a phone number that answers on the first ring.
