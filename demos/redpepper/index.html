Our Story

Three generations.
One fire.

The Red Pepper started in 1987 when Yiayia Maria turned her courtyard into a taverna. The grill has never gone cold. Today her son Andreas runs the kitchen and her grandson Dimitris runs the floor.

617 reviews later, we still use the same charcoal, the same olive oil, and the same recipe for lamb chops that Maria wrote on a napkin in 1987.
