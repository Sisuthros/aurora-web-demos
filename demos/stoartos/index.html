Our Story

Three years
to get it right.

Artos means bread in Greek. StoArtos means "to the bread." When we opened in 2021, we had one sourdough starter, one coffee machine, and a belief that Larnaca deserved better bread.

611 reviews later, we're still at it. The starter is stronger. The coffee is better. The queue on Saturday mornings still stretches to the corner.
