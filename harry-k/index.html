About

One chair.

Harry opened his chair in 2018 with a simple belief: a barbershop should be a sanctuary. Not a factory. When you're in the chair, you're the only person in the world.

Old-school tools. Straight razors. Hot towels. Real scissors. No electric clippers where they don't belong. This is barbering the way it was meant to be — slow, deliberate, masterful.
