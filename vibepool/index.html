Our Origins

From Óbidos to the world

VibePool was founded in Óbidos, Portugal — a medieval town where history meets innovation, and where taking time to think clearly has always been valued. The castle walls that have stood for centuries remind us: what lasts is built with intention.

We bring a European sensibility to AI — one that prioritises ethics, sustainability, and long-term thinking alongside technical excellence. Technology should serve human purpose, not the other way around.
