Our Origins

From Óbidos to the world

VibePool was founded in Óbidos, Portugal — a place where history meets innovation, and where taking time to think clearly has always been valued.

From this intentional starting point, we work with organisations globally. Our perspective remains grounded: technology should serve human purpose, not the other way around.

We bring a European sensibility to AI — one that prioritises ethics, sustainability, and long-term thinking alongside technical excellence.
