Our Story

Three generations of taste

In 1976, Andreas opened a small fish taverna on the Larnaca seafront. The fishing boats docked steps away, and the day's catch went straight from net to grill. Word spread — locals came for the Fish Meze, tourists stayed for the sunset.

Today, the third generation runs Zephyros. The boats still dock outside. The fish is still fresh. The meze still brings people together. Some things don't need to change.
